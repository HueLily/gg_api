Women in Computer Science

TIGERHACKS 2023

Overview

General Information

TigerHacks is a 14-day hackathon organized by Women in Computer Science in collaboration with Tenzer Technology Center. It is an exciting and challenging opportunity for DePauw students to level up their Computer Science, teamwork, and presentation skills. This hackathon is open to all DePauw students and is designed to best support the community of women in CS at DePauw.

Our GOAL

Our goal is to provide an opportunity for DePauw students, especially women in CS at DePauw, to collaborate, learn, and innovate while fostering a supportive and inclusive environment. Come join us for a fun-filled 2 weeks of coding, networking, and growing!

Announcement

During the two weeks of the hackathon (October 23rd - November 6th, 2023), we will host workshops and office hours to support participants with their projects.

Requirement

Attendance at the Kick-Off Session (5:00 pm, October 23rd in JSC 147) is REQUIRED in order to officially register to compete. All important information about the hackathon, including the prompt/main topic, will also be delivered in this session.

ELIGIBILITY & TEAM FORMATATION

  • TigerHacks is open to all students who are currently enrolled at DePauw University, regardless of gender, class year, and major.
  • Participants can compete either individually or as teams of up to 3 members. However, male participants cannot outnumber female participants. In other words, teams of two must have at least one woman, and individual submissions are accepted by female participants only.
  • Participants are required to attend the Kick-Off Svent to officially register for the hackathon.
